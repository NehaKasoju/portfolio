Neha Kasoju

Mechatronics Engineering Student

Blending advocacy, healthcare, and technology, my journey has always been rooted in equity. From researching gender-affirming care to building self-driving systems and founding grassroots initiatives, I've sought to design solutions that serve underrepresented communities.

From creating a braille printer to improve accessibility for young learners, to designing surgical tools that empower marginalized communities, and reshaping education policies as a Student Trustee, I'm passionate about using innovation to make a difference.

I thrive on hands-on projects, tackling complex challenges, and driving real impact through empathy-driven tech solutions.
